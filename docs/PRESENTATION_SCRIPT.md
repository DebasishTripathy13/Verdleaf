**Q: How do you prevent people from taking photos of the same bottle twice?**
**A:** "Great question. We use image hashing to detect duplicates, and we also use metadata (GPS and timestamp) to ensure the action is unique and recent."

**A:** "Speed and Cost. Ethereum is too slow and expensive for a consumer app where you might mint an NFT every day. Solana's Compressed NFTs allow us to mint millions of assets for pennies."
