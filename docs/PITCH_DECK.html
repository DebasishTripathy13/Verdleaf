The Grind

Challenges we overcame to build Verdleaf.

AI Hallucinations

Problem: Gemini sometimes identified non-recyclable items as recyclable.

Solution: Implemented "Few-Shot Prompting" with specific negative examples to improve accuracy by 30%.

Gas Fees

Problem: Minting NFTs for every user action was too expensive and slow.

Solution: Switched to Solana Compressed NFTs (cNFTs) to reduce cost to near-zero ($0.000005).

User Retention

Problem: Users might get bored after the initial novelty wears off.

Solution: Added "Streak Mechanics" and "Rare Evolution Paths" to incentivize daily logins.
